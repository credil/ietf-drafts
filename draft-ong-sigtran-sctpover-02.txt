        RFC 3286

        Title:      An Introduction to the Stream Control Transmission
                    Protocol (SCTP)
        Author(s):  L. Ong, J. Yoakum
        Status:     Informational
        Date:       May 2002
        Mailbox:    lyong@ciena.com, yoakum@nortelnetworks.com
        Pages:      10
        Characters: 22644
        Updates/Obsoletes/SeeAlso:  None

        I-D Tag:    draft-ong-sigtran-sctpover-02.txt

        URL:        ftp://ftp.rfc-editor.org/in-notes/rfc3286.txt


This document provides a high level introduction to the capabilities
supported by the Stream Control Transmission Protocol (SCTP).  It is
intended as a guide for potential users of SCTP as a general purpose
transport protocol.

This memo provides information for the Internet community.  It does
not specify an Internet standard of any kind.  Distribution of this
memo is unlimited.
