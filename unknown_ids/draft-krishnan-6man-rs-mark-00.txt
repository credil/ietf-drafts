Internet-Draft          Line Identification in RS           October 2008


Abstract

   In ethernet based aggregation networks, several subscriber premises
   may be connected to the same interface of an access router.  This
   document proposes a method for the access router to identify the
   subscriber premises using the contents of the received router
   solicitation messages.
